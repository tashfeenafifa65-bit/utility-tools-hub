How to Use the Case Converter

Our free Case Converter helps you quickly transform text into different letter formats. Simply enter or paste your text, choose the desired case style, and instantly get the converted result.

Available Text Case Formats

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capital letters.
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word.
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence.
  • Toggle Case: Switches uppercase letters to lowercase and vice versa.
