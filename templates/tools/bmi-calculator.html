What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple measurement that uses your height and weight to estimate whether your body weight falls within a healthy range.

How to Use the BMI Calculator

Enter your weight in kilograms and your height in centimeters. Click the Calculate BMI button to instantly view your BMI score and category.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: Below 18.5
  • Normal Weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30 and above

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a healthy BMI?

A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is generally considered healthy.

Is BMI accurate?

BMI is a useful screening tool but does not directly measure body fat percentage.

Can athletes have a high BMI?

Yes. Athletes may have higher BMI values because muscle weighs more than fat.
